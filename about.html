About Our Hotel

CHECK-IN 12 PM . CHECK-OUT 10 AM
King Hotel provides air-conditioned accommodation in Lonavala. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. Free private parking is available and the hotel also offers car hire for guests who want to explore the surrounding area. At the hotel, each room comes with a terrace with a city view. King Hotel Lonavala all rooms have a wardrobe, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Buffet and la carte breakfast options are available every morning at the accommodation. Mumbai is 50 km from King Hotel King, while Navi Mumbai is 40 km from the property. The nearest airport is Chhatrapati Shivaji International Mumbai, 37 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
